## 🏥 **PROFESSIONAL VETERINARY GUIDELINES**

### **Storage Requirements:**
- **Temperature**: 2-8°C (refrigerated storage)
- **Protection**: Keep away from light and heat
- **Expiry**: Check expiration dates before use

### **Administration Techniques:**
- **Drinking Water**: Mix with clean, cool water; ensure all birds have access
- **IM Injection**: Use proper needle size; inject into breast muscle
- **SC Injection**: Inject under skin in neck area; avoid blood vessels

### **Safety Protocols:**
- **Sterile Equipment**: Use clean, sterilized needles and syringes
- **Proper Technique**: Follow manufacturer instructions
- **Monitoring**: Watch for adverse reactions post-vaccination
- **Record Keeping**: Document all vaccinations and responses
