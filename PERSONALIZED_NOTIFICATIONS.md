### **Vaccine-Specific Preparation Notes** ✅
Each vaccine includes specific preparation instructions:

- **Newcastle Disease**: Store at 2-8°C. Use within 2 hours of reconstitution.
- **Infectious Bronchitis**: Mix with clean, cool water. Ensure all birds have access.
- **Gumboro Disease**: Use sterile equipment. Administer via drinking water or injection.
- **Fowl Pox**: Use wing-web method. Ensure proper needle sterilization.
- **Fowl Typhoid**: Administer via subcutaneous or intramuscular injection. Monitor for adverse reactions.
- **Marek's Disease**: Administer subcutaneously in neck. Use within 2 hours of reconstitution.
- **Avian Influenza**: Follow strict biosecurity protocols.
